About Me

Christine is a 2015 UC San Diego graduate who received her B.S. in Computer Science. She currently works at Cisco as a Software Engineer in the Collaboration Technology Group. During her first year at Cisco, Christine has mentored new hires and has shown them how to take advantage of the resources at Cisco and in the broader Silicon Valley region. She also teaches Scratch coding to low income students at a local middle school once a week. Christine enjoys traveling and during her last semester at UCSD, she studied abroad in Edinburgh, Scotland and took advantage of the opportunity to explore Europe.
